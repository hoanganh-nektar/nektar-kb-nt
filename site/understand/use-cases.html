Use cases

Ways customers use Nektar Transform to auto-populate engagement scores, multi-threading levels, activity dates, and more.

Nektar Transform lets you populate any Salesforce field — built-in or custom — using full-featured mini programs that run within Nektar. Here are the ways customers are using transforms today.

Engagement Score

Quantifies the depth of engagement by analyzing the recency, frequency of communications, and other relevant signals.

Available at an Account, Opportunity, Lead, Contact and Opportunity Contact Role level.

Multi-threading level

Measures the number of unique contacts engaged within an account or opportunity, indicating the breadth of relationships across stakeholders.

Available at an Account and Opportunity level.

First/Last Email/Meeting Date

Tracks the initial and most recent communication dates to highlight engagement timelines and identify potential gaps.

Available at an Account, Opportunity, Lead, Contact and Opportunity Contact Role level.

Count of Emails/Meetings in Last x days

Quantifies recent interactions to assess activity levels, helping to spot active deals or accounts at risk due to low engagement.

Available at an Account, Opportunity, Lead, Contact and Opportunity Contact Role level.

Conditional Creation

Restricts the creation of contacts and opportunity contact roles under accounts and opportunities based on engagement levels, engagement types, and other predefined criteria, ensuring only relevant records are added to the CRM.

Available at Contacts and Opportunity Contact Role level.

Role/Type Identification

Auto-classifies contacts into roles like Economic Buyer, Champion, or Influencer based on job titles and engagement behavior, aiding in stakeholder mapping. Also auto-classifies emails and meetings into types like Demo meeting, Business Review, or Cadence call based on subject lines, body content and stakeholders involved.

Available at Opportunity, Opportunity Contact Role, and Event level.

Job Change

Detects when key contacts switch companies, allowing timely updates to CRM records and proactive relationship management.

Available at Contact level.
