Tracker

Trace why an email or event was or wasn't synced.

Tracker lets you look up the path of a specific activity through Nektar's data processing pipeline. It helps understand why Nektar might have excluded a certain activity from Salesforce.

You may search for an event or email by keywords in its subject line and/or the email addresses of participants.

No results?

An activity that was read by Nektar might not show up in the tracker for two reasons:

  • It is outside the time window: the tracker only displays activities that were read by Nektar more than 2 hours ago but less than 30 days ago.
  • The activity was filtered out by a read rule.
